About Us

Our school mainly aim at improving the child both academically and outside the world

The idea of Maryland international school was born in 2005 under the "Dennis christian fellowship". In 2005, the government of Uganda signed and sealed the commitment deed confirming its donation of land in Kampala city for the development of maryland international school. This contribution was fifteen hectares of land and the developments on it at kampala hill. In the same year, an application to operate Maryland international school as a centre of higher learning was submitted to the Ministry of Education and Sport. Maryland international school is located on plot 34 block 5 kampala hill, in kampala city about two kilometer off entebbe express highway. the main campus sits on 13 hectres of land, the school can be accessed via nabagereka road which connects the kampala campus to the highway.
Maryland international school has a great potential of being the leading international school, given its location and opportunity of offering quality education to the world, there by enhacing social and economic intergration
